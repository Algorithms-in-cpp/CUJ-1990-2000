How to Get SPLASH

The full source code for SPLASH can be obtained from the USENET by anonymous ftp from netcom.com

For UNIX-type filenames, download:

~ftp/pub/morris/splash.tar.Z

For DOS-type filenames, download:

~ftp/pub/morris/splash.zoo

Zip format for DOS will be available on the October 1993 CUJ code disk.

SPLASH has also been posted to alt.sources and will soon appear in comp.sources.misc.

If you can't access any of the USENET archive sites, you can e-mail me at morris@netcom.com. I will e-mail you either a split sh archive or a uuencoded compressed tar format.
