SKY Computers Debuts SKY MPI-RT

SKY Computers has released SKY MPI-RT, an MPI-RT library based on the MPI-RT defacto standard. The standard specifies real-time multiprocessor communications routines that simplify development and provide cross-platform portability of multiprocessor application code. MPI-RT provides a standard for the library of C/C++ subroutine calls used to ensure cross-platform and cross-vendor portability.

SKY Computers has implemented all of the syntax of the MPI-RT standard and provided a facility to layer older MPI standard code on top of MPI-RT. The SKYchannel 44-bit address space gives MPI-RT a large, flat address space.

SKY MPI-RT starts at $7,000 for a development license. A run-time license costs $1,400.

For more information, contact SKY Computers, Inc., +1.978.250.1920; fax +1.978.250.0036; info@sky.com; www.sky.com.

WaveMakers Research Announces WaveMakers Speaker Identification Software

WaveMakers Research has announced WaveMakers Speaker Identification Software. WaveMakers Speaker Identification Software can distinguish between members of a group by voice characteristics, regardless of what is being said and regardless of the language being spoken. Using a single, omnidirectional microphone, WaveMakers Speaker Identification Software can identify speakers at distances of up to 10 feet.

In addition to identifying who is speaking, WaveMakers Speaker Identification Software requires less than 70K of RAM, uses less than four-percent of the CPU cycles of a Pentium 350, requires two minutes of training time per voice, and one to two seconds of voice data to identify a speaker.

JK microsystems has released µFlashTCP, a web server and TCP/IP controller. With preinstalled DOS, NE-2000 packet driver, and web-server software, µFlashTCP helps add TCP/IP connectivity to embedded products.
